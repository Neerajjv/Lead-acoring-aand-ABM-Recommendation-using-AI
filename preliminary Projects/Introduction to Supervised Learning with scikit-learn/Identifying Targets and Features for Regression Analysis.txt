Q . Which of the following scenarios represents a suitable regression target variable?


Predicting the type of flower species based on petal length and width.

Estimating the time it takes to complete a marathon race.

Classifying whether an email is spam or not.

Identifying whether a patient has a certain medical condition.

Ans : Estimating the time it takes to complete a marathon race.


Q. In which of the following situations is regression the most appropriate technique?


Determining the sentiment (positive/negative) of a movie review.

Forecasting the price of a house based on its features.

Categorizing images of animals into different species.

Predicting whether a customer will buy a product.


Ans : Forecasting the price of a house based on its features.
